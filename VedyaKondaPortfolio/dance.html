Kuchipudi Dancer

I had my formative and foundational training under Guru Smt. Himabindu Challa and am now pursuing advanced training at the ShivaMohanam institute of Kuchipudi under Smt. Padmavani and JaiKishore Mosalikanti.

- 2017 National YoungArts Foundation Winner

- 2018 IndianRaga Kuchipudi Fellow

- 2018 Saptami Foundation National 1st prize winner in Kuchipudi

Research Project

Apprenticeship Program, Alliance for California Traditional Arts’ (ACTA):

- Awarded a stipend to understand the connection between modern day expression and their roots in traditional art forms

- Researching the connections between the modern day classical dance form that I practice called Kuchipudi and the 3rd century text titled Natyasastra, which gave birth to the classical dance forms of India.

- Learning the movements described in the Natyasastra from Surabhi Bharadwaj, a practitioner and expert of these movements, to deepen my knowledge.
