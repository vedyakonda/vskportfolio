A Bit More About Me!


My name is Vedya Spurthi Konda and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I'm constantly learning new things and love meeting people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world.

I'm a senior at Santa Clara University where I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Art History. I am always looking for ways to combine my design, research, technical, and leadership skills in an internship. More specifically, I'm interested in UX Design, UX Engineering, UI/UX design, Product design, and AR design. I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design and code and am constantly looking to learn new things every day.
