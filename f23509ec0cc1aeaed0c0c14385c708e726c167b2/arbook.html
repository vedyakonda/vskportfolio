Oh, Baby, the Places You’ll Go

To help kids develop a love for reading and imagination through an Augmented Reality version of Dr. Suess’s Oh, Baby, the Places You’ll Go.


OVERVIEW

Along with Santa Clara University's Imaginarium, I am working on an Augmented Reality version of the book Oh, Baby, the Places You’ll Go. This book uses a technology called Unite AR to help bring the pictures Illustrated by Dr. Suess to life through live animation.

Books are portals to other dimensions and can encourage and enhance a child's imagination and creativity. I velieve that Dr. Suess's books are the best examples of books that help broaden children's horizons and chose one of his books as my focus. I also believe that while Dr.Suess's books sometimes have complex language embedded with alliteration and metaphors, the text helps put everything in perspective. I hope to use Augmented Reality to bring emphasis to the pictures and encourage more kids to read.

TOOLS
